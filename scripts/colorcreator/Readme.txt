How to use:

Step 1:Insert 6-Digit Hex codes in colors.txt (Color per line NO SPACES)
Step 2:Run the Powershell Script (Might be prompted to allow scripts to run)
Step 3:Copy colors folder to %Appdata%\Kodi\addons\skin.pcxl\media (If you want you can delete the orginal folder)
Step 4:Copy and replaces Include_Colors.xml to %Appdata%\Kodi\addons\skin.pcxl\16x9
Step 5:Restart Kodi or reload skin
